THE TAJ MAHAL




  1. The Taj Mahal is a beautiful white marbel monument located in the city of Agra on the banks of the river Yamuna.
  2. It is one of the finest examples of architecture that combines elements of Mughal,Persian,Ottoman Turkidh and Indians architecture style.
  3. Located in the state of Uttar Pradesh, it is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a jewel of Muslim art in India.
  4. It was build by the Mughil Emperor Shah Jahans in memory of his wife, Mumtaz.
